ABOUT THIS GAME

Drive your car and smash into other vehicles to destroy them and win the race! The more opponents you destroy, the more points you get. Play with friends or random players from all over the world. Challenge yourself to get as far as you can and reach the finish line first.

The game is completely free to play.

TrackRacing Pursuit is a multiplayer car racing game that includes a lot of elements that make it unique and fun.

This game is all about cars and driving, so if you are a fan of these things, then you will enjoy this game. There are so many cars that you can choose from and drive. The cars include real-life vehicles.
